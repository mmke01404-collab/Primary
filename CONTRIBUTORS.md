## How to Get Listed

Contributions that lead to a **merged pull request** or a **substantive, acknowledged discussion** may be added to this file.

Types of contributions that qualify:

- ✅ **Philosophical refinement** — New examples, counter-examples, or edge cases for the Humming Box Problem
- ✅ **Technical implementation** — Verification scripts, lineage tooling, manifest automation
- ✅ **Documentation improvements** — Clarifications, translations, or structural fixes to core files
- ✅ **Standards integration** — Mapping Lineage-Primary to PROV-O, PREMIS, OAIS, or other models
- ✅ **Case studies** — Real-world preservation failures or successes that inform the framework
- ✅ **Legal or archival expertise** — Contributions to provenance statements or copyright clarity

**What does NOT qualify:**
- ❌ Spam, off-topic comments, or code of conduct violations
- ❌ Trivial typo fixes (though they are appreciated)
- ❌ Requests for access to private source materials

## Requesting to Be Added

If you believe you have made a qualifying contribution and are not listed:

1. Open an **issue** with the label `contributor-request`
2. Include:
   - Link to your contribution (pull request, discussion thread, or issue comment)
   - Brief explanation of why it meets the criteria above
   - How you wish to be named (real name, pseudonym, or organization)

The maintainer will review and respond within 14 days.

## Removal from Contributors

A contributor may be removed if:

- They request removal (privacy or personal reasons)
- They violate the `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` repeatedly
- Their contribution is later found to be plagiarized or fraudulent

Removals will be documented in commit history (no retroactive deletion).
