The Humming Box Problem

Your files are doomed — but not for the reason you think. Digital preservation is not only about protecting bits. It is about protecting meaning, context, interpretation and continuity.

A file can remain technically intact while its identity becomes unreadable. The Lineage-Primary Framework proposes that continuity of meaning, not preservation of objects, is the true unit of long-term survival.
